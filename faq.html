Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Do you offer delivery?

Yes. Delivery is available via Bolt or Yango. Delivery fees are paid directly to the rider.

Can I pay on delivery?

No. All food orders must be paid online via Paystack.

What areas do you deliver to?

Haatso, UPSA/Legon, Madina, Atomic, Achimota, and nearby areas.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders cannot be cancelled once preparation has started.

How do I contact you?

You can contact us via WhatsApp for support or order issues.

Is my payment safe?

Yes. All payments are securely processed by Paystack.
